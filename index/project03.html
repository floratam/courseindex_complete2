project 03 - me of the screen

prompt

this project asked us to design an infographic through self-observation and documentation as design-based research, especially with how we interact with digital tools and environments, for example, interfaces. it also requires that we record pieces of both quantitative and qualitative data and represent it in a space of our choosing.

concept

my idea was to observe the way i interact with spotify, something that is very integral to my daily routine and everyday life. i thought this would be an ideal interface to examine as it can provide many quantitative data points and qualitative, as music is very emotional and subjective. i sought out to thinking of what information i wanted to record and observe, as well as the best way to display this information afterwards.

since i use my noise cancelling headphones often when i listen to music, i wanted to incorporate that element into the research. because the noise cancellation is often too strong for my liking, i like to have it on the "transparency" or ambient sound setting, allowing me to have bits of the noise around me in, along with whatever song i am listening to at that moment. because of this, i wanted to record both the "outisde" noise and "inside" noise, to show the contrast between the two environments.
